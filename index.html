Our Boba Journey

Founded in 2018, Boba Bliss began as a small passion project between two friends who shared a love for authentic boba tea. We traveled across Asia to learn traditional brewing techniques and source the finest ingredients.

Today, we're proud to bring you handcrafted boba drinks made with premium teas, fresh milk, and house-made boba pearls. Every cup is prepared with care to deliver the perfect balance of flavor and texture.

Our mission is simple: to create moments of happiness, one sip at a time.
